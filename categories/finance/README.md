# Finance - AI Topics

This directory contains research from **FT50 and top finance journals** on **AI topics** in finance.

## Focus

Papers about **AI's impact on financial markets, institutions, and services** - not papers that merely use AI as a tool.

## Topics Covered

- AI in Financial Markets
- AI and Market Efficiency
- AI and Trading (algorithmic trading, HFT)
- AI and Financial Stability
- AI in Financial Services (fintech, banking, insurance)
- AI and Credit Markets
- AI and Market Structure

## Key Journals

- Journal of Finance (JF) - FT50
- Journal of Financial Economics (JFE) - FT50
- Review of Financial Studies (RFS) - FT50
- Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis (JFQA)

## Example Papers

- "Artificial Intelligence and High-Frequency Trading" (JF)
- "Machine Learning and Market Efficiency" (RFS) - if it discusses AI's impact
- "Fintech, Regulatory Arbitrage, and the Rise of Shadow Banks" (JFE)

## Notes

- Only FT50/top-tier finance journals
- Focus on AI topics, not AI methods
- Papers must examine AI's impact on finance
