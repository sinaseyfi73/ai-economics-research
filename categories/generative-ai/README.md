# Generative AI & Large Language Models

Research from **FT50 journals** on **generative AI and LLMs** in economics and finance.

## Topics

- Generative AI at Work
- ChatGPT and Productivity
- Large Language Models in Economics
- Generative AI and Creative Work
- LLMs and Decision-Making
- AI Assistants and Labor

## Key Papers

- **"Generative AI at Work"** (QJE, 2024) - Brynjolfsson, Li, Raymond
  - Impact of ChatGPT on customer support productivity
  - 14% productivity increase
  - Largest gains for less experienced workers
