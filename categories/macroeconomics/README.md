# Macroeconomics - AI Topics

This directory contains research from **FT50 and top economics journals** on **AI topics** in macroeconomics.

## Focus

Papers about **AI's impact on aggregate economic outcomes** - growth, productivity, business cycles, inflation.

## Topics Covered

- AI and Economic Growth
- AI and Productivity Growth
- AI and Business Cycles
- AI and Inflation
- AI and Labor Markets (aggregate)
- AI and Capital Formation
- AI and Total Factor Productivity

## Key Journals

- American Economic Review (AER) - FT50
- Quarterly Journal of Economics (QJE) - FT50
- Journal of Political Economy (JPE) - FT50
- Review of Economic Studies (RES) - FT50
- Journal of Economic Literature (JEL) - FT50
- Journal of Economic Perspectives (JEP) - FT50

## Example Papers

- "Artificial Intelligence and Economic Growth" (NBER)
- "The Productivity Paradox of the New Digital Economy"
- "Artificial Intelligence and Productivity" (JEL)

## Notes

- Only FT50/top-tier economics journals
- Focus on AI topics (AI's impact), not AI methods
- Macroeconomic perspective required
